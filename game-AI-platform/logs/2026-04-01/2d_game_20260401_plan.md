### 游戏描述

A classic snake game with retro pixel art aesthetics. Players control a snake that grows longer as it consumes food items scattered across the screen. The challenge increases as the snake's length makes navigation more difficult, requiring careful planning to avoid colliding with walls or the snake's own body. Features include score tracking, progressive difficulty, and nostalgic 8-bit visual style reminiscent of early arcade games.
